### Irreducible Subset:
A [[DES - Definition of 'Closed Subset'|closed subset]] $S \subseteq \mathcal{X}$ is **irreducible** if every state $j \in S$ is [[DES - Definition of 'Reachable State'|reachable]] from any other state $i \in S, \kern 15px i \neq j$
